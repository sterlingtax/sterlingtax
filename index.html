Not sure where to start? Try a Projection Snapshot.

A one-time look at where this year is heading, while there is still time to act. Includes a current-year projection, a one-on-one review, a withholding check and a written summary. If you upgrade, it credits toward Sterling Forecast.

$350 one-timecredits toward Forecast

Plans are tailored to your situation and pricing varies with complexity. Sterling Forecast is a recurring plan billed semiannually and includes federal and state return preparation. Business entity returns, audit representation, tax resolution and advanced multi-state work may be separate services unless included in your engagement agreement.

Privacy Policy

How Sterling Tax & Accounting, Inc. collects, uses and protects your information.

Overview

Sterling Tax & Accounting, Inc. ("Sterling," "we," "us") respects your privacy and takes the protection of your information seriously. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it and the choices you have. By using our website or our services, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide to us directly, such as your name, contact details, and the financial and tax documents needed to prepare your returns or provide accounting and planning services. We also collect basic, non-identifying information about how visitors use our website so we can improve it.

How we use your information

We use your information to prepare and file returns, provide accounting and planning services, respond to your questions, communicate with you about your work with us, and meet our legal and regulatory obligations.

How we share your information

We do not sell your information. We share it only as needed to provide our services, for example with the secure portal and software providers we rely on, with your consent, or when required by law. Where we work with our partner Silver Grove Financial Group on your behalf, we do so consistent with this policy.

How we protect your information

We use a secure client portal and reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You can ask to review or update the information we hold about you, or ask questions about how your information is handled. We will do our best to help.

Other websites

Our site links to other websites, including our partner Silver Grove Financial Group and our portal provider. Those sites have their own privacy practices, and we encourage you to review them.

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, email us at info@sterlingtax.com or reach our corporate office at 400 Sterling Drive, Orchard Park, NY 14127.
